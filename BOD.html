Key roles in the club

There are few key roles every Rotary Club will have and these are listed as below.

Role Responsibilities Appointed Member
Executive Secretary Manage club correspondence, including responding to emails and sending official notices and invitations. Preserve your club's historical records. Write an annual report for the club at the end of the Rotary year. Rtn. Munirathnam Javaji
Immediate Past President (IPP) It shall be the duty of the Immediate Past President to serve as a Director and to perform such other duties as may be prescribed by the President or the Board. In addition, the Immediate Past President shall preside at the meetings of the Club and the Board in the absence of the President and President-Elect. Rtn. E L Narayan Reddy K P
Mentor As a mentor, you will help a new member to understand more about Rotary and to engage in our club. A mentor is an important role in the long-term retention of a new member! Getting a new member fully engaged in Rotary is an important job. Rtn. Ramesh Bhat
Sergeant at Arms The function of the sergeant-at-arms is to help maintain an orderly, dignified, and effective Rotary club meeting, one that will make the right kind of impression on club visitors and guests. Rtn. Chandrashekar N
President Elect (PE) The President-Elect's primary responsibilities focus on preparing for his/her year as Association President. As such, s/he oversees and is directly involved in Association strategic planning, and By-Laws management. S/he serves as a member of the Finance Committee. Rtn. Munirathnam Javaji

Board of Directors

ROTARY BANGALORE SAHAKARNAGAR (RBS) president selects the board for the current rotary year. Appointed Directors serve for a term of one year. The primary responsibility of all directors is to contribute toward the success of the vision, goals and activities of the Club. They are expected to delegate, coordinate, and monitor various Club operational functions and assure that they are properly accomplished.

Board of Directors, their Roles and Responsibilities are as below

Role Responsibilities Appointed Member
Club Administration Planning and organizing weekly meetings and programs. Promoting fellowship among club members. Keeping members informed through a club newsletter or Web site. Rtn. Ravindra G
Membership Building a positive, welcoming club environment for all members. Participating in the district vibrant club workshop and the district training assembly. Rtn. Manjunath K P
